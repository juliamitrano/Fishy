Fishy

Goal: Eat all the fish you can and become the biggest fish in the pond.

Instructions:
	•	Move to other fish to eat them.
	•	Eating fish will make you bigger.
	•	Win by being the biggest fish in the pond.

Rules:
	•	You must be bigger than a fish to eat it.
	•	Fish can eat you too so BE CAREFUL.
	•	You will lose if you get eaten.

Controls(Keyboard):

	“Right” - move right
	“Left” - move left
	“Up” - move up
	“Down” - move down
	“X” - quit game
